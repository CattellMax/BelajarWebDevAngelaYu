The Best Movies According to Cattell

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sore

A quiet and emotional film about memories, love, and moments that linger in everyday life.

Ultraman Rising

An action-packed sci-fi story that reimagines Ultraman with modern visuals and a heroic journey.

Kimi No Nawa

A beautiful and touching anime about fate, time, and two souls mysteriously connected.
